Quick answer

The Bocce Standards Official Size 107mm set is our top pick for its genuine tournament-grade feel and excellent craftsmanship.

For budget buyers, the Sport Squad 90mm set offers solid play at a low price, while the Hathaway set is perfect for families who want versatility.

Bocce ball is one of the oldest lawn games, and for good reason: it’s easy to learn, social, and works on grass, sand, or dirt. A good set can last for decades, so picking the right one matters more than the price tag. When choosing a set, focus on ball size (90mm is casual, 107mm is regulation), material (solid resin is best, filled plastic is cheaper and lighter), and overall weight. The case or bag is another factor, especially if you plan to travel with it. The sets below are ranked for playability, durability, and value across a range of budgets.
